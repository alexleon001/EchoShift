## Full Description — English

EchoShift is a cyberpunk-styled pattern puzzle game. Memorize sequences of lights on a grid, replicate them in order, and face increasingly difficult patterns powered by adaptive AI.

FEATURES:

— ADAPTIVE AI —
Claude AI generates unique patterns that adapt to your skill level. No two games are alike.

— COMBO SYSTEM —
Chain correct taps to multiply your score up to x10. One mistake breaks your streak.

— 4 GAME MODES —
• Endless: No limits, how far can you go?
• Blitz: 60 seconds, max score
• Daily: One pattern per day, everyone competes
• Duo: Real-time 1v1 with friends

— 5 POWER-UPS —
Freeze, Hint, Undo, Slow, and Shield. Spend Echoes strategically to survive.

— GENERATIVE AUDIO —
Music that reacts to your combo. The beat intensifies as you play better.

— SOCIAL —
Global leaderboards, friend system, public profiles, and shareable score challenges.

— CUSTOMIZATION —
Grid skins with unique visual effects. Unlock them with Echoes.

Free to play. Compete. Dominate.
